About TomamuX

Your Perfect Getaway in Hokkaido

Nestled in the heart of Hokkaido’s breathtaking winter landscape, TomamuX offers a unique stay-and-ski experience for travelers seeking adventure, relaxation, and stunning mountain views. Our ski-in/ski-out apartments provide direct access to the slopes, making it the perfect destination for ski enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Ski-in/Ski-out Access, Cozy Duplex Apartments, Ice Village & Minna Minna Beach, Family & Adventure-Friendly

At TomamuX, we blend adventure with relaxation, offering a home away from home in one of Japan’s most spectacular winter destinations. Whether you’re here for an exhilarating ski holiday or a peaceful retreat, TomamuX promises an unforgettable experience.

    One mountain, One purpose

    Mount Tomamu
    (トマム山)

    Location

    Located in the Hidaka mountains in Hokkaido, Japan.

    By Train

    Hourly JR limited express trains run between Sapporo and Tomamu Stations.

    The trip takes around 90-100 minutes, costs about 4500 yen.

    Free shuttle buses operate between the Tomamu Station and the Village Alpha.

    By Bus

    From Chitose Airport
    ADULT 4100 yen
    CHILD 3100 yen

    By Car

    2.5 hours drive from Sapporo city. Car rental is available from the airport or different prefectures.

    It is a great way to see Hokkaido. Visitors would need international driving licenses.
